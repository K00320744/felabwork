Understanding the Environment

The environment includes all living and non-living things on Earth. Clean air, fresh water, fertile soil, and rich biodiversity are vital to our survival. However, human activity is putting immense pressure on our planet.

Tackling environmental issues like climate change and pollution requires action at every level — personal, local, and global. Small steps taken today can create a sustainable future for all.

Learn more from reliable sources like: Britannica – Ecology

  • Protect endangered species and habitats
  • Switch to renewable energy sources
  • Support local, organic produce
  • Reduce single-use plastics
  • Join or support environmental groups
